This project focuses on improving user experience by optimizing and enhancing features such as photo and real-time conversation translation. We aimed to inject fresh perspectives into this familiar tool, We hope to make the user experience smoother and simpler, reducing language barriers between people.
1. Our Goal
The goal is to offer users a more intuitive and efficient translation experience. This will be achieved through streamlining the interface, improving the overall functionality of the translation features, and adding details that better suits user habits.

6. Usage Analysis
Accessibility issue with homepage history: Users are unable to directly access their browsing history from the homepage. They must navigate to it by clicking on the icon at the top while editing text.
The image translation process is cumbersome and fails to showcase Google Search's exclusive advantages: Users must go through four interactions to view image translation results, including selecting the image, scrolling to the bottom, clicking on "Search," and then "Open in other apps." Users often translate to understand the actual content's meaning, and surface-level text translation cannot fulfill this need. Google Search's potential and advantages in this regard are prominent, so it should be more easily discovered and used efficiently by users.
Inconvenience of Conversation Translation Mode: The conversation translation mode only displays one person's translation result at a time, and the record before starting a new recording disappears, lacking contextual support.
Limitations of the Bookmark Feature: The bookmarked content can only be sorted by time or first letter, lacking support for organizing by category or other criteria.
