 Journeys:
‍
My journey began with a Bachelor’s degree from Beijing University of Technology, where I distinguished myself by participating in an exchange program at Tsinghua University. During this time, I had the opportunity to collaborate on various design projects and assist in organizing art events.

I further honed my skills with an internship that cooperate with Weta Digital, where I contributed to designing visual elements for a museum exhibit in collaboration with the Chinese Academy of Medical Sciences. This experience allowed me to work on a diverse range of tasks, from creating presentation PPTs to designing interactive museum components.

In my professional career, I’ve been privileged to work as a visual designer for companies like ZiTu Publishing Company and ZhuiJi Intelligent Technology. I designed and dozens of books, and over 300 mobile phone screens, collaborated with 22 brands, and created captivating promotional materials. My work has been recognized with several awards.

I’m deeply committed to continuous learning and creativity. My diverse skill set includes graphic design, video editing, H5 production, and even game design. I thrive on exploring new tools and methodologies, always eager to push the boundaries of design.

Beyond my technical skills, I’m a dedicated team player who values responsibility, innovation, and effective communication. I look forward to bringing my unique perspective and extensive experience to exciting new projects, creating designs that not only meet but exceed expectations.
